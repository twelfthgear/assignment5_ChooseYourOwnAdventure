The front door gives way as you open it slightly. Quietly, you crawl over the furniture.

You make it over a couch, a couple of lawn chairs, half a ping pong table, and then you trip. You foot caught on some soft of cable.

Across the room you hear a clash of cans and commotion come from behind a door. You take a second to listen in. "Crap, shit. God dammit, I don't wanna die to zeds, oh god why."

And then you realize what is happening. Something catastrophic has happened and it's bad, something out of a Sci-Fi show.

You call out to the person behind the door. "Hello? I'm not a zombie, I promise."

The shuffling inside from behind the door stops and you hear a muffled response.

"Y-You promise?"

You stifle a laugh as you promise you're not a zombie. Soon after you hear more shuffling and from behind the door you see a short, curly haired boy peek his head out.

He is wearing a much too oversized football helmet with your college's colors and is carrying a broomstick with a butterknife duct taped to the end.

"Who are you?" the boy asks. You could ask the same but explain that you are a student.

"Oh... So you know what happened then?"
"What?"
