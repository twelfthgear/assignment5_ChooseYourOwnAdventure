As you make your way to the campus, you notice the air is surprisingly quiet. No traffic or birds chirping, just eerie silence. The grass is ripped up, most of the lower level windows of the nearby buildings have been busted in, including the glass doors. The cafeteria looks untouched, but the main entrance is barricaded with chairs and tables. The library looks untouched.
